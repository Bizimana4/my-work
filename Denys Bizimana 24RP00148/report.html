This report provides insights into the performance and status of Baby Register Rwanda over the past year. The following data highlights the number of registrations and trends observed throughout the year.

Conclusions

Based on the data presented, we can observe a steady increase in registrations, with a peak in May. The growth is attributed to increased public awareness of the Baby Register platform and enhanced outreach efforts during the first quarter of the year.
